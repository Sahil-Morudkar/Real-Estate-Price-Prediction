What do we do?

Real Estate Property is not only the basic need of a man but today it also represents the riches and prestige of a person. Investment in real estate generally seems to be profitable because their property values do not decline rapidly. Changes in the real estate price can affect various household investors, bankers, policy makers and many. Sales forecasting has always been a very significant area to concentrate upon. Manual infestation of being able to predict House Prices could lead to drastic errors leading to poor management of the organization, and most importantly would be time consuming. This webapp helps you do all of that, Just enter your requirements and get to know the price of the house you're looking for.

The following values in the dataset will give you a clear insight

Under_Construction : 1 = It is Under Construction, 0= It is not Under Construction

Rera Approved : 1 = It is Rera Approved, 0 = It is not Rera Approved

Bedroom_Hall_Kitchen or Room_Kitchen : 1 = BHK , 0 = RK
